Job: IT Support Analyst and Consultant

SUMMARY

The IT Support Analyst and Consultant will be working directly with our clients and provide technical support for existing systems and technical advice for future oriented plans.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Provide customer service support on-site or via phone, email for technical and non-technical users
  • Gather and document system requirements
  • Understand and troubleshoot technical problems
  • Desktop installation and support
  • Escalate issues to higher support engineers

REQUIRED EXPERIENCE AND QUALIFICATIONS

  • Diploma in Information Technology related field
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal, and problem-solving skills
  • Knowledge with remote network and PC support
  • Understanding of Microsoft and Linux based operating systems, anti-virus/anti-spyware, backup & recovery methods, and PC & basic network hardware required.
